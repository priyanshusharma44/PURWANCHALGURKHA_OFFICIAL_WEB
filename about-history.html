Founded in 2019, Purwanchal Gurkha Security emerges as Nepal's preeminent security provider, proudly drawing strength from a legacy crafted over two decades in the esteemed Indian Army. Our journey begins with a commitment to excellence, rooted in the traditions of the Gurkha regiment.

2019

 

A New Chapter Begins

Established with a vision to redefine security standards, Purwanchal Gurkha Security commenced its journey in 2019. Fueled by a commitment to safeguarding our clients, we embarked on a path of excellence that continues to shape our identity.

2020

 

Evolving to Meet Your Needs

In our unwavering commitment to meet the evolving needs of our clients, we expanded our service portfolio in 2020. This milestone is a testament to our dedication to excellence, driving us to enhance our capabilities continually. Our goal is to provide comprehensive security solutions tailored to your unique requirements.

Branching Out for Accessibility

A significant milestone in 2021 marked the establishment of our inaugural branch office. This strategic expansion reflects our unwavering commitment to enhancing accessibility, responsiveness, and service delivery for our valued clients.

Pinnacle of Excellence

In 2023, Purwanchal Gurkha Security stands tall as the pinnacle of excellence in the security services industry. Our journey continues to evolve, driven by a steadfast commitment to providing unparalleled protection and innovative solutions.
